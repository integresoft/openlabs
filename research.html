  • Exploring Human Nerves

    SCIENCE   Trevor B. Bondy, Founder of OPENLABS - May 24, 2023

    The nervous system is the body system that is responsible for the communication and processing of human sensory information, and is responsible for responding to these messages. The nervous system allows communication between the different parts of the body and between major body organs, allowing the control of the body and reception of information. Sensory receptors in the senses send messages to the brain, which can then process the information and could possibly send a response back into the body.
